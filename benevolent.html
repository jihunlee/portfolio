Benevolent Validator Portal

Service Design Project


Benevolent is a crowdfunding platform where people in needs post video of their request with help of non profit validators and get donations from online donors. We created Benevolent validator portal to provide validators, who are easily forgotten from this help equation, a sense of belonging and an access point to the Benevolent community with Profile, Needs, Network, and News features.
