Samsung Happy Bank

Interaction design project

Happy Bank is a platform that allows users to travel through their happy experiences and later share them with others. This was a solution to Samsung mobile's proposal to design a future interaction with mobile devices. Happy Bank help users capture, organize, and share their memorable contents on their smart phone device.

1 2 3 4

Design Process

Contextual Interview

With "Happiness and Entertainment" being our constraints for the project, we conducted a series of contextual interviews to understand what those topics meant for them. We created an interview guide that let us carefully probe what users perceive "Happiness and Entertainment" without we having to ask any direct questions.

Design Direction & Ideation

After collecting stories from users, the team created an overarching design direction with highest resonance. Our design direction was 'How might we provide users with relevant content, smart organization, and frictionless sharing?' We brainstormed ideas on multiple rounds and also created storyboards that we thought had potential. As I was in charge of visual design, I drew all the storyboards as well as detailed scketches during this stage.

Prototype & Iteration

As finalized our concept, we conducted an experiment with software silverback to validate our hypothesis of using smile as a trigger of the interaction. We then created rough sketches of wireframe and tested UI flow with users using POP. After iterations of POP exercises, we made higher fidelity UI wireframes. We also made a working prototype to demonstrate our capturing aspect of the concept with Processing.
