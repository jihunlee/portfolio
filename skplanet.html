SK Planet WASSUP DOG

Start-up Business Model Generation

WASSUP DOG is a mobile LBS crowdsourcing platform to support stray dogs in South Korea. WASSUP DOG utilizes LBS, allowing users to search dogs near them in order to increase the likelyhood of physical interaction and emotional connetion. Users can support their dogs by donating, visting & playing, and bragging (advertising) on SNS. Finally, when visiting dogs, by using NFC tagging, users can identify themselves and give notifications to animal hospital doctors who provide shelters for stray dogs

Design Process

Contextual Interview

After conducting desk research, I led my team to go out and perform context interview. This process not only helped gain insights in creating our concept, but also motivated team members to fully commit to help stray dogs in Korea

Design Direction & Ideation

With stories that we collected during interview process, I led my team a series of brainstorming sessions to collectively create a concept that addresses painpoints and unmet needs of users

Prototype & Iteration

During the project, I was in charge of creating a complete scenario of the WASSUP DOG App. I also participated in final GUI production process as well.
