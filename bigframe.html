Bigframe Market Opportunity

Design Strategy Project

I am not allowed to talk about details of the project due to non disclosure agreement

Big Frame is a Multi Channel Network that focuses on bringing YouTubers (Channel Subscribers)
and advertisers together


Personal Contributions to the project

Research / Data analytics

Conducted desk research through sources such as ComScore and Northwestern archive to gather data in order to evaluate candidate markets. I was also in charge of interviewing and extracting insights from Youtube viewer’s behaviors and extracted pain points and unmet needs of Bigframe compared to Youtube.


Design Direction

Recommend a solution that was not initially asked from the client. From the research summary, I recommend a market that had a lot of potential in terms of expansion with low risk of entrance. The team agreed with the direction that I proposed, and the client was pleasantly surprised and agreed to pursue actively with the final recommendation that team proposed.


Ideation

Created mock-up scenario of the website from the recommendation that we made. Even with a small set of wireframes of the website containing three new feature recommendations, I was able to communicate clearly with the client during the final presentation via video-conference.
